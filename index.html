Downsize.js

This library is for quick and easy image downsizing with sudo antialiasing

Download View examples

Downsized with 4 steps

Good smoothing, can blur image

var canvas = document.getElementById("..");
Downsize({
  canvas: canvas,
  src : "demo/assets/img1.jpg",
  steps : 4,
  size: 0.25
});

Downsized with 2 steps

Moderate gains, 2 passes

Downsizes is easy to use!

Resize your image in steps

Resizing an image down directly from 100% can cause graphical glitches. Downsize.js does multiple interpolations with fewer pixels per step to make images smoother
